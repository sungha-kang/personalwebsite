Hi! My name is Sungha, and I'm a Ph.D. student at the University of Maryland. My research focuses on how people navigate and engage with health information in digital environments, and how we can better support informed decision-making online.

Since 2021, I’ve also worked as a metadata researcher, helping make data more FAIR—Findable, Accessible, Interoperable, and Reusable— to support a more transparent and structured data ecosystem.
